About DevHaven Studio

I build digital experiences that help brands teach, sell, launch, and get taken seriously online.

I am Olamide Joshua Olawuyi, CEO and lead developer behind DevHaven Studio. I build modern websites, conversion-focused pages, and digital products that stay sharp across phones, tablets, laptops, and large screens.

I started building in 2018, then committed to coding seriously in 2019. Since then I have worked across responsive business websites, ecommerce pages, academy experiences, portfolio systems, landing pages, and sales-focused funnel builds that help people understand the offer quickly and take action with confidence.

One of my most hands-on projects was a school CBT portal for exams. Building that system deepened my backend experience with PHP and MySQL, and sharpened the way I think about real workflows: authentication, question management, timed sessions, scoring logic, and admin control.

I also enjoy shaping practical digital experiences around offers and growth, from ClickFunnels-style landing flows and WhatsApp-first enquiry journeys to academy checkout pages, payment integrations, and brand sites that need better structure, stronger messaging, and cleaner UI.

Today my work sits at the intersection of design clarity, frontend execution, and practical problem solving. I build for clients through DevHaven Studio, and I teach through DevHaven Academy so people can learn skills that lead to real projects, real delivery, and real confidence.
