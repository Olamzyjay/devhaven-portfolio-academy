About DevHaven Studio

I help schools, founders, and small teams launch clean, responsive websites that people understand quickly.

I am Your Name, CEO and lead developer behind DevHaven Studio. I build websites that feel clear, fast, and trustworthy on every device.

I started building websites in 2018. By 2019, I committed to coding seriously and began turning that skill into real products: landing pages, school sites, and systems that help people take action.

One of my most hands-on projects was a school CBT portal for exams. Building that system strengthened my backend fundamentals with PHP and MySQL, and taught me how to think about real users: student logins, question management, timed assessments, scoring, and admin control.

My focus became simple: I kept seeing great businesses and great schools lose enquiries because their websites were confusing, slow, or hard to use on a phone. I wanted to fix that with clean structure, readable design, and dependable code.

Today I split my time between building for clients and teaching practical skills through the DevHaven Academy so beginners can ship real pages, not just notes.
